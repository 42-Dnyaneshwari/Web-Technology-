The college is permanently affiliated to the University of Pune and is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi. Various programs offered here have already been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). Alumni of the Institute are holding very good positions in reputed firms both in Government and Private sectors. Quite a good number of passed out students are either employed or perusing higher studies abroad also. On the top of it, Many students of our institute secure top positions in the university merit list.
